Example: How to Use Data Extractor

  1. First Upload your PDF of Prescription or Patient Name
  2. Prescription Should Must Contain
  3. Patient_name, Patient_address, Medicines, Directions, Refills. For Best Results
  4. For Patient Details ---
  5. Patient_Details Should Must Contain
  6. Patient_name, Phone_number, Medical_problems, Hepatitis_b_vaccination
  7. Enjoy your Extracted Data!!!

That's it! Hopefully this example helps you understand how to describe steps.
